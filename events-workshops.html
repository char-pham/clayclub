Events

When: At the end of very month


Unique, planned sessions focused on social community, special demonstrations (e.g., guest artists), or themed celebrations.

Workshops

When: Biweekly and often with two part sessions


Structured, instructor-led lessons focused on building specific technical skills, such as glazing, throwing, or hand-building.

Studio Drop-In

When: Twice a week


Unstructured, self-guided time where you use our materials to practice techniques and create projects at your own pace.
