Our Mission


Our mission is to foster a vibrant, low-stress community that breaks down the financial barriers to ceramic arts, offering all students the resources and instruction needed to transform clay into personal expression.

Our Values


Accessibility - We believe in access to affordable materials

Community - We build a supportive and stress-free space

Growth - We offer a balance of structured learning

Expression - We provide a vital outlet for creative ideas and personal decompression
